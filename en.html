Hidden Bar in Ishigaki Island

Concept

Cafe&Bar Adam and Eve is a small cocktail bar located in Hamazaki, Ishigaki Island. You can enjoy cocktails, whisky, awamori and other drinks in a relaxed atmosphere. A quiet counter-style bar that is easy to visit alone or with friends.
